About Move 5430: The Short Version

Move 5430 is all about fitness for people in pain.

It was created by Cody and Cara, two physical therapists, who also happen to be married.

We believe that pain and injury can act as a wake up call for you to reconnect with movment and your body's natural strength and resilience.

We aim to provide a sophisticated understanding of the interactions between pain, injury, strength, resilience, fitness and health.

Further, we want to provide the tools necessary for you to take ideas and put them into action.

After all, knowing what to do is the easy part.

Doing it consistently enough for long enough to see change is the hard part.

Last, we want to test our ideas. Many web sites collect all kinds of data to figure out how to sell you more stuff. We want to take the data from this community and use it to help the community make better, faster progress.
