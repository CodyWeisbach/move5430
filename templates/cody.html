Me in 10 seconds

I'm a physical therapist with an interest in programming and data science.

I'm scientifically minded and quite skeptical of a lot of the pain solutions out there. I'd rather help people understand their problem and focus on doing what we know works with focus and intent.

Here are a few of my biggest influences...

  • David Butler and the Neuro Orthopedic Institute for all things pain
  • Ido Portal via Boulder Movement Collective for all things movement and my own training.
  • Frank Forencic from Exuberant Animal for a broader view of health and enjoying life.
  • Functional Movement Systems for smart systems and interesting corrective exercise.
  • Most importantly a ton of studies related to pain, movement, injury, recovery and performance.

I'm a Boulder, CO native and live there with my wife Cara, son Parker, and daughter Chloe.

We like playing in the outdoors, music, and spending time with friends and family.
